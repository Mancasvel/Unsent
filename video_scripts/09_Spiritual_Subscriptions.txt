
Video 9 – “The Price of a Soul”

Hook:
How much would you pay to understand your own heart?

Body:
I struggled with how to price Unsent. It felt wrong to put a paywall on healing. But it also costs money to keep the lights on, to run the AI that makes this possible.

So I didn't create pricing tiers. I created spiritual gateways.

You can start for free, with a whisper. A glimpse. But if you want to go deeper, to have those conversations with the AI ghosts, to truly excavate... that requires more energy. From the servers, and from you. It’s not a subscription. It’s an investment in your own inner world.

Final punch:
You can’t buy peace of mind. But you can pay for a map to find it.
